Bio

All Hail the Boognish

Buckingham Ween is Southern California's premier tribute band dedicated to celebrating the experimental and wildly creative music of Ween. Founded in 2019, the band captures the raw energy, technical prowess, and comedic spirit of one of rock's most innovative acts.

With meticulous attention to both the studio versions and the improvisational live performances of Ween's vast catalog, Buckingham Ween delivers an authentic and passionate experience for longtime fans and curious listeners alike.

About

The Boognish Awaits

Buckingham Ween is Southern California's premier tribute band celebrating the psychedelic rock genius of Ween. Known for their technical mastery and faithful interpretations, the band commands the stage with intensity and reverence for one of the most creative forces in modern rock.

"The songs remain the same, but every note is a prayer to the Boognish." — Buckingham Ween

From the whimsical pop confections of "The Golden Eel" to the devastating prog-metal of "Transdermal Celebration", Buckingham Ween recreates the full spectrum of Ween's legendary output with passion, precision, and the playful spirit that defines the band's philosophy.
